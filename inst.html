Instructions

  1. 1. Main objective of the game is to aim and defeat the green pigs by launching birds towards them.
  2. 2. Tap and drag the bird back to aim and adjust the power of the shot.
  3. 3. Release your finger to launch the bird.
  4. 4. To complete a level, you must destroy all the green pigs before running out of the birds.
  5. 5. If you fail to defeat green pigs, game will be over.
  6. 6. You can restart the game at any time.
